FAQ

Common questions

Who can become a Slrint affiliate?
Anyone. There's no follower count requirement, no approval process, no qualifications needed. If you know Amazon sellers — agencies, freelancers, consultants, influencers, friends — you can refer them.
How does the 20% recurring commission work?
When someone signs up using your link and pays for Slrint, you earn 20% of every payment they make — month after month, year after year. As long as they stay subscribed, you keep earning.
What's the cookie window?
30 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 30 days, they're credited to you. We use last-touch attribution — if they later click another affiliate's link before signing up, they'd be credited to that affiliate instead.
When and how do I get paid?
Payouts happen monthly on the 5th. Once your accumulated commission crosses ₹1,000, we transfer it to your bank account via NEFT/UPI. Below ₹1,000, it rolls over to next month.
Do I need a GST registration?
Not required if your total annual commissions are below ₹20 lakh. If above, you'll need to provide a GST number to receive payments (we'll provide a Form 16A for TDS).
Can I refer myself?
No. Self-referrals and referrals to immediate family members are not eligible for commission. We monitor this and disqualify abuse — you'd lose your affiliate status.
What if the customer cancels their subscription?
You stop earning commission for that customer. There's no penalty or clawback — you keep all commissions earned while they were subscribed. If they re-subscribe later, you start earning again (within 12 months of original referral).
Can I run ads to my affiliate link?
Yes — with restrictions. You can run ads on social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter). You cannot bid on "Slrint" or related branded keywords on Google Ads. You cannot impersonate Slrint or claim to be an official representative.
