Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the Slrint affiliate program

How does the 20% commission work? ▼
You earn 20% of every payment your referred user makes. If they pay ₹4,999/month for Growth, you earn ₹1,000/month — every month they stay subscribed. It's truly recurring.
What is the 30-day cookie window? ▼
When someone clicks your referral link, a 30-day cookie is set. If they sign up and pay anytime within 30 days of clicking your link, you get credited for the referral.
When do I get paid? ▼
You can request a payout once your pending earnings reach ₹1,000. We process payouts within 3–5 business days via UPI or bank transfer.
Who should I refer? ▼
Slrint is built for Amazon India sellers — brands, D2C founders, private label sellers, and Amazon ads agencies. If you run a blog, YouTube channel, or community for Amazon sellers, this is a perfect fit.
Is there an approval process? ▼
No. Any registered Slrint user can join the affiliate program instantly. Just log in to app.slrint.io, go to the Affiliate tab, and your referral link is ready.
Do I need to be a Slrint customer? ▼
Yes — you need a free Slrint account to access the affiliate dashboard and generate your referral link. You can sign up for free at app.slrint.io.
